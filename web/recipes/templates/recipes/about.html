About Our Tool

Hi! This is Joy! Welcome to Green Gourmet Guide!

Our tool identifies vegetables from your images and suggests delicious recipes to ignite your cooking passion.

Inspired by everyday grocery challenges, it's designed to simplify meal planning and enhance the cooking experience for novices and seasoned food enthusiasts alike.
